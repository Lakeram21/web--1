Design Principles

White Space

Amazon.com

Amazon

White Space- Amazon's webpage has a lot of items and a lot of information. However becausse of their use of white space, their page is attractive, readble. They do a great job by placing space between items and items information so that a customer is aware which information belongs to which item. White space is the blank area around, inside, or between the elements of a design. It’s not the star of the show, but great design cannot exist without it.

Contrast

Walmart.com

Walmart

Contrast is designing a distinct difference between foreground and background colors in order to enhance readability. Walmart is a great example of this. A customer can easly read, naviagate and search for items on their website becasue the background color makes the fonts higly readable.
